Welcome to Dance Beat Studios

Your home for inspiring dance classes in Rustington, where every student can grow, perform and shine.

With expert teaching in Ballet, Tap, Jazz, Modern and Zumba, we help dancers of all ages build strength, confidence and creative expression.

Come join Sally and the Dance Beat Studios family for friendly classes, strong technique, and a welcoming studio experience.

Our Dance Classes

Ballet

Structured ballet classes for students of all ages. Training includes technique, performance preparation, and ISTD work.

Jazz

Energetic jazz classes that build confidence, style, and performance skills with creative choreography.

Tap

Tap lessons covering rhythm, footwork, and technique for beginners through improvers.

Modern

Modern dance classes focused on expression, movement exploration, and contemporary performance work.

Zumba

High-energy fitness class combining Latin and international music with dance moves. All abilities welcome.

Private Lessons

One-to-one tuition for exam preparation, performance work, or focused skill development.

Stretch Class

Dedicated stretching and flexibility training for dancers. Improve your range of motion, prevent injuries.

Dance Beat Tots

Fun introduction to ballet, tap, and modern dance for ages 3-5. A playful way for young children to develop coordination.

Core and More

Core and More classes focus on building core strength, flexibility, and overall fitness through targeted exercises.
